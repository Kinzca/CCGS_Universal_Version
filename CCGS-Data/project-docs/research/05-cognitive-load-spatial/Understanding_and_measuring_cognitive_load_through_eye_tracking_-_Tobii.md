# Understanding and measuring cognitive load through eye tracking

[Download your free guide](https://www.tobii.com/resource-center/reports-and-papers/understanding-and-measuring-cognitive-load-through-eye-tracking#cta-section)

## Summary

Every decision, lesson, and task places demands on our limited working memory. When the mental effort — or cognitive load — is too high, performance drops. Too low, and engagement suffers.

This comprehensive guide explores how eye tracking provides a real-time, non-intrusive way to measure cognitive load and unlock insights into learning, decision-making, and performance.

## What's inside

A clear introduction to cognitive load theory

A comparison of measurement methods (self-report, performance, physiological)

How eye metrics like pupil dilation, fixations, and saccades reveal mental effort

Practical tips for research in both labs and real-world settings

Real-world applications in education, UX, healthcare, and safety
